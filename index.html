Max Olan Smith

I am senior research scientist at Field AI.

I received my Ph.D. from the University of Michigan, where I was advised by Michael P. Wellman. I have also had the pleasure of working with the Game Theory team at DeepMind, Paris and at the Montréal Institute for Learning Algorithms.
